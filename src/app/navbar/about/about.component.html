Our History

JPack Int’l Inc. is a family-owned business in the City of Industry that is a master distributor of packaging and shipping materials. Established in 1998, we have grown through the years because of our company’s focus on high quality products, excellent services, and low prices. As a family business, our employees and founder pay extreme attention to detail and will do everything in their power to satisfy your demands. It is the norm for our founder to personally visit and serve customers and for our employees to know consumers by name and accommodate to individual needs. We have members of the JPack family who speak fluent Mandarin as well as English to meet your communication needs and make your company’s shipping process as convenient, hassle-free, and low-cost as can be.
